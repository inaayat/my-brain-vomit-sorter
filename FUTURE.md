# Future Features

Ideas and improvements to explore in future versions. These are captured from brainstorming sessions within the app itself.

---

## High Interest

- **Mind map visualization** — Visual graph showing connections between items and clusters
- **Prioritization arrows** — Simple up/down arrows on action items to indicate priority order
- **Note-taking within items** — Richer text editing, markdown support, longer-form notes attached to items

## Medium Interest

- **"Go back" navigation** — Navigate to the most recently viewed page/tab
- **Effort categorization** — Tag items as "quick task" vs "big exercise" to help with planning
- **Due date reminders** — Surface overdue/due-today items more prominently
- **Export/share** — Export wins, clusters, or item lists as markdown/PDF

## Exploration

- **Multi-device sync** — Sync database across devices (iCloud, or git-based)
- **Keyboard-driven workflow** — Full keyboard navigation without mouse
- **AI braindump sorting** — Paste a long braindump, AI splits it into individual items
- **Tags view** — Browse items by AI-generated tags
